## Notes

- Under Tennessee cottage food law, you list generic ingredient names (not brand names)
- Ingredients should be listed in order of predominance (most to least by weight)
- Fresh/perishable products must be sold in-person only
- Frozen products can be sold via any method (online, markets, etc.)
