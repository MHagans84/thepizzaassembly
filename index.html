How It Works

From kit to pizza in 4 simple steps

1

Rest the Dough

Remove from fridge 1-2 hours before baking

2

Stretch

On semolina, push from center outward with fingertips

3

Top It

Add sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings

4

Bake

450°F, bottom rack, 10 minutes until golden
